1A. Coating Flat-Bottom Plates

  1. Add 120 µL cold dPBS + 0.1% BSA per well (1 well/sample). No bubbles at bottom.
  2. Incubate ≥1 min at 4°C
  3. Remove at 120 µL, then 10 µL to get all excess — residual liquid interferes with assay
  4. Store coated plates at 4°C until use

1B. CPair Preparation

  1. Tap tube on bench (never vortex). Pipette at 150 µL, 10× until homogeneous. Avoid warming with hands. Do not let solution sit for more than 30 sec before next step
  2. Transfer to 1.5 mL tube: 170 µL (8-sample kit) or 340 µL (16-sample kit)
  3. Magnet 1 min RT → remove sup (150 / 300 µL). Pellet is small — slides down!
  4. Wash: off magnet → +300 µL cold dPBS, pipette 5× → magnet 1 min → remove 300 µL
  5. Resuspend: +150 µL (8) or 300 µL (16) cold dPBS, pipette 10×
  6. Into pre-coated wells: 20 µL (Primary Tissue) or 15 µL + 5 µL dPBS (Cell Lines)
  7. Cover, store at 4°C — do not freeze. Unused washed good for 1 month at 4°C.

  1. Quick spin . Mix with pipette at 600 µL (8) or 900 µL (16) until homogeneous
  2. is extremely viscous — pipette VERY slowly. No bubbles! Use reverse pipetting.
  3. Prepare : 73.6 µL + 6.4 µL 1M DTT per sample. Mix slowly.
  4. Aliquot 80 µL per tube into strip tubes, place on ice

1D. Cell Priming

  1. Cells: 250 cells/µL (cell lines, 5K total), 425/µL (PBMCs, 8.5K), 500/µL (organoids, 10K) in 20 µL dPBS+0.04% BSA
  2. Reconstitute : +200 µL ice-cold dPBS → vortex 30s → quick spin = 6×
  3. Dilute: 1× (20 µL 6×+100 µL dPBS) cell lines/PBMCs | 1.5× (30+90) organoids | 2× (40+80) primary
  4. Add 6 µL diluted to cells in V-bottom, mix 5× at 20 µL. Incubate on ice 20 min.
  5. Wash 1: +175 µL dPBS+3.75% BSA → mix 5× at 150 µL → 500×g, 3 min, 4°C → remove sup (see for technique)
  6. Wash 2: +200 µL dPBS+3.75% BSA → spin → remove sup (see for technique)
  7. Wash 3: +200 µL dPBS (NO BSA) → spin → remove all sup (see for technique) + residual with 20 µL tip
  8. Resuspend in 20 µL cold dPBS, mix 10×
  9. Transfer to flat-bottom with . Mix 20× at 20 µL — tilt plate, aspirate bottom/sides, dispense top
  10. Cover. Settle 40 min on ice — VIBRATION-FREE!
